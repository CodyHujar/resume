About

Hello, and welcome to my resume site.

This is not a standard portfolio page as I wanted to focus specifically on my accessibility knowledge and skills.

You are highly encouraged to navigate my website via keyboard and/or screen reader.

I wanted to highlight how familiar and comfortable I am with utilizing vanilla markup languages like HTML and CSS. This website utilizes a minimal amount of Javascript for menu navigation and ease of access needs such as visibility toggles and scroll-to-top buttons.

Javascript adds a lot of weight to a page and creates bloat on the Internet. For individuals on slow connections or utilizing mobile devices on anything below 5G, removing Javascript helps improve their experience navigating the web while also reducing global energy demand. Limiting Javascripted is a net positive to users and creators alike.

If you'd like to see some of my work that heavily utilizes Javascript and frameworks like React, please visit my current employer's homepage.

You can also check out the Packout Builder, an application I put a lot of focus into making accessible. You can visit the standalone version of the application here.

Enjoy your stay and I hope we get to meet soon!
